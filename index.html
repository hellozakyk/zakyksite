Who do you make logos for??

We create beautiful, high-quality logos for all kinds of clients – start-ups, big businesses, nonprofits, podcasts, students, and everything in between. As long as you need a logo, we have you covered!

What information do I need to provide to start my project?

On the order page, you'll complete a brief questionnaire asking about your logo name, industry, target audience, preferred styles, and any specific ideas or inspirations you might have. We'll take care of the rest!

How long does the logo design process typically take?

Our standard logo design projects are typically completed within 3 business days, depending on any revision rounds and feedback speed.

How do revisions work?

We offer flexible revision options to make sure you absolutely love your new logo! After we show you concepts, just give us your feedback, and we'll get straight to refining the designs based on what you tell us!

What file formats will I receive for my final logo?

You'll get your logo in all essential formats to make sure it looks great everywhere! We provide the original source file for any future edits. You'll also receive scalable vector files (SVG) and common image files (JPG, PNG) for your website, social media, documents, etc.
